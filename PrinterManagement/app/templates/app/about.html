Filaments:

Filament Prices vary a fair bit for anything outside of PLA and ABS, Wood, Copper, Brass and Steel can be had for a reasonable price, Carbon Fibre, PETG, and Nylon should be exclusively used for prints that require specific strength such as RC parts that would typically be too weak made of PLA.

Types of Filament Available:

  • PLA - Standard filament used, strong though it has a low melting temperature
  • ABS - Stronger than PLA, slightly flexible, better heat resistence
  • NYLON - Extremely Durable, heat resistent, and chemical resistent
  • PETG - Very Durable, more flexible than both PLA and ABS
  • Carbon Fibre - Light weight very high strength material, expensive to print with
  • PLA Infused (Wood, Copper, Brass, Steel) - infused with particles of metal or wood for asthetics

All jobs will be quoted and printed in PLA unless requested, any other material will be extra cost

All jobs will be quoted and printed in Black, White or Grey unless requested, other colours may increase the price
